La Commune Bookstore Cafe

La Commune is a collectively-run bookstore & cafe providing literature and sustenance to the Omni community

La Commune will run a bookstore & cafe the way they ought to be run—collectively!

As the front door of the Omni, we will welcome visitors with a hot or cold drink & something interesting to read.
